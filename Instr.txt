                                                 
                                               << How to Play: >>
 

Choose either one of the games, Hangman or CPS-Test


-> How to Play HANGMAN:

          The computer generates a word by random and you have to guess 
          the word either letter by letter, or the whole word itself, you get a 
          total of 6 tries before the hangman dies and your game is over.
          Click Retry if you want to try again


-> How to play CPS TEST:

          You have 5 seconds to click as fast as possible.
          The countdown starts as soon as you click the button
